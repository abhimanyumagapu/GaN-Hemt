This is a 2-D model of the GaN hemt.

Starting from the bottom:

Silicon substrate - 12x5nm - 1e14 boron doping
AlN - 12x0.02nm 
GaN - 12x2nm - 1e15 arsenic doping
AlGaN - 12x0.05nm - 5e16 phosphorus doping
GaN source - 1.5x1nm - 5e20 arsenic ohmic doping
GaN drain  - 1.5x1nm - 5e20 arsenic ohmic doping
Gan gate - 2x1nm - 1e16 boron doping

Passivation layer 1 (Si3N4) - 2.5x1nm
Passivation layer 2 (Si3N4) - 4.5x1nm
